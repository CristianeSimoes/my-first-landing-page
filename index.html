All Roads Lead to Code!

There are three ways you can become a Web Developer. The most traditional one is to pursue a college degree, which can take 3 to 4 years. But if you already have a degree or are eager to start working, you can choose to attend a Bootcamp or an Academy. These options are excellent for accelerating your learning and preparing you for the job market.

  • 1
    The most traditional one is to pursue a college degree, which can take 3 to 4 years.

  • 2
    If you already have a degree or are eager to start working, you can choose to attend a Bootcamp or an Academy. These options are excellent for accelerating your learning and preparing you for the job market.

  • 3
    Another option is to explore the world of coding as a self-taught individual, by taking online courses, watching tutorials on YouTube, and participating in challenges and hackathons.

  • No matter which path you choose, having a portfolio with at least 3 robust projects will be essential. You will find these and many more tips in our e-books.
